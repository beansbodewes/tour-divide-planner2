How To Use This Site

  1. Create a username and password so your plan auto-saves and you can come back anytime.
  2. Pick a route (or choose Create Your Own Ride), then set projected days and build your plan.
  3. Use your own research to adjust daily mileage, stops, and resupply details.
  4. Check the map and elevation tabs to confirm resupply and stop points, and read comments for current section info.
  5. Export CSV to share your plan.
